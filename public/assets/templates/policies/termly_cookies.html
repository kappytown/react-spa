1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site. 3. Types of Cookies We Use

The cookies used on our Site can be categorized as follows:

A. By Duration

  • Session Cookies: These are temporary and expire once you close your browser. They allow us to link your actions during that specific browsing session.
  • Persistent Cookies: These remain on your device for a set period specified in the cookie and are activated each time you visit the Site. They help us remember your preferences for future visits.

B. By Origin

  • First-Party Cookies: These are set by the website you are visiting (i.e., by [Your Website Name]).
  • Third-Party Cookies: These are set by a domain other than the one you are visiting, usually from external services embedded on the site (e.g., Google Analytics, social media platforms, or advertisers).

C. By Purpose

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: These cookies are essential for you to browse the Site and use its features. They cannot be turned off.
  • Analytical/Performance Cookies: These allow us to recognize and count the number of visitors and see how visitors move around our Site.

  • Targeting/Advertising Cookies: These record your visit to our Site, the pages you have visited, and the links you have followed. We use this information to make our Site and the advertising displayed more relevant to your interests.
